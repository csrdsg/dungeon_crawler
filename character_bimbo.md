## Character Background

### Description
Bimbo is a charming rogue with a silver tongue and nimble fingers. Despite a frail constitution, he makes up for it with wit, charisma, and an uncanny ability to talk his way out of (or into) trouble. His low constitution means he avoids direct combat when possible, preferring stealth and cunning.

### Personality Traits
- Charismatic and persuasive
- Quick-witted but physically fragile
- Prefers sneaking to fighting
- Excellent at social manipulation

### Goals
- Survive dangerous dungeons through cleverness
- Acquire wealth and reputation
- Avoid getting hit (seriously, that CON score...)

## Build Notes

**Strengths:**
- High Charisma (+1) makes him great at negotiations and social encounters
- Balanced DEX allows for decent stealth and initiative
- Well-rounded rogue skill set (Stealth, Lockpicking, Trap Detection)

**Weaknesses:**
- Very low Constitution (5) means only 20 HP - extremely fragile!
- No combat bonuses - must avoid direct fights
- Average attributes overall except for CHA

**Playstyle:**
- Avoid combat when possible
- Use Persuasion (CHA +1) to talk out of fights
- Rely on stealth and traps to control encounters
- Let tougher allies tank while you support
- Focus on treasure hunting and trap disarming

**Recommended Progression:**
- Level 2: Increase Stealth to Rank 2, add Persuasion Rank 1
- Level 3: Max Lockpicking to Rank 2, save points for synergy
- Level 4: Aim for Stealth Rank 3 & Lockpicking Rank 3 for Rogue's Expertise bonus
- Consider increasing CON at next attribute increase opportunity!
