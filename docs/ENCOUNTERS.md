# Dungeon Encounters

## Overview
Encounters are events, creatures, or NPCs that adventurers may meet while exploring dungeon chambers. Encounters are categorized by their disposition: Friendly, Neutral, or Hostile.

---

## Friendly Encounters

### 1. Lost Merchant
**Location:** Any chamber
**Description:** A frightened merchant who got separated from their caravan. Offers discounted goods in exchange for escort to safety.

### 2. Trapped Adventurer
**Location:** Prison Cells, Trap Rooms
**Description:** Fellow adventurer caught in a trap or imprisoned. Will join party temporarily if freed.

### 3. Hermit Wizard
**Location:** Library, Empty Room
**Description:** Reclusive mage living in the dungeon. Offers magical knowledge or enchantments.

### 4. Escaped Prisoner
**Location:** Prison Cells, Empty Room
**Description:** Innocent person wrongfully imprisoned. Knows secret passages and chamber layouts.

### 5. Friendly Ghost
**Location:** Library, Throne Room
**Description:** Spirit of former dungeon inhabitant. Provides warnings about dangers ahead.

---

## Neutral Encounters

### 1. Scavenger Goblins
**Location:** Empty Room, Armory
**Description:** Small group of goblins picking through debris. Will trade or flee rather than fight.

### 2. Ancient Golem
**Location:** Throne Room, Puzzle Room
**Description:** Magical construct following old orders. Won't attack unless provoked or commanded.

### 3. Dungeon Fauna
**Location:** Any chamber
**Description:** Giant rats, bats, or beetles. Dangerous if cornered but mostly avoid contact.

### 4. Wandering Spirit
**Location:** Any chamber
**Description:** Ghostly apparition reliving past events. Ignores living creatures unless disturbed.

### 5. Rival Adventuring Party
**Location:** Treasure Room, Boss Chamber
**Description:** Another group of explorers. May cooperate, compete, or demand tribute for passage.

### 6. Cursed Knight
**Location:** Armory, Throne Room
**Description:** Undead warrior bound to protect the dungeon. Attacks only those who steal or vandalize.

### 7. Underground Trader
**Location:** Empty Room, Prison Cells
**Description:** Mysterious merchant dealing in rare goods. Fair but asks no questions.

### 8. Magical Familiar
**Location:** Library, Puzzle Room
**Description:** Lost wizard's familiar seeking its master. Helpful if treated kindly, mischievous otherwise.

---

## Hostile Encounters

### 1. Orc War Band
**Location:** Throne Room, Monster Lair
**Description:** Aggressive orc warriors claiming the dungeon as territory. Attack on sight.

### 2. Giant Spider
**Location:** Monster Lair, Empty Room
**Description:** Massive arachnid with poisonous bite. Ambushes from ceiling or shadows.

### 3. Skeletal Guards
**Location:** Throne Room, Armory, Prison Cells
**Description:** Undead soldiers still following their ancient orders. Mindless but coordinated.

### 4. Dungeon Bandits
**Location:** Treasure Room, Armory
**Description:** Criminal gang using the dungeon as hideout. Demand valuables or attack.

### 5. Gelatinous Cube
**Location:** Any chamber
**Description:** Transparent ooze that dissolves organic matter. Slow but deadly.

### 6. Dark Cultists
**Location:** Throne Room, Puzzle Room
**Description:** Fanatics performing forbidden rituals. Attack intruders to protect their secrets.

### 7. Rust Monster
**Location:** Armory, Empty Room
**Description:** Creature that corrodes metal on contact. Attracted to weapons and armor.

### 8. Gargoyles
**Location:** Throne Room, Boss Chamber
**Description:** Stone creatures that animate when intruders approach. Guard important areas.

### 9. Zombie Horde
**Location:** Monster Lair, Prison Cells
**Description:** Shambling undead in various states of decay. Slow but numerous.

### 10. Mimic
**Location:** Treasure Room, Armory
**Description:** Monster disguised as treasure chest or furniture. Ambushes greedy adventurers.

### 11. Fire Elementals
**Location:** Trap Room, Boss Chamber
**Description:** Living flames bound to the dungeon. Hostile to all non-magical beings.

### 12. Mind Flayer
**Location:** Library, Throne Room
**Description:** Psychic horror that feeds on brains. Uses mental domination in combat.

### 13. Beholder
**Location:** Boss Chamber, Monster Lair
**Description:** Floating aberration with deadly eye rays. Paranoid and aggressive.

### 14. Vampire Lord
**Location:** Boss Chamber, Throne Room
**Description:** Undead noble ruling the dungeon. Commands lesser undead and uses dark magic.

### 15. Ancient Dragon
**Location:** Boss Chamber, Treasure Room
**Description:** Powerful wyrm guarding its hoard. Breathes fire and casts spells.

## Encounter Guidelines

### Frequency by Chamber Type
- **Empty Room:** 30% chance of encounter
- **Treasure Room:** 60% chance (usually hostile)
- **Monster Lair:** 90% chance (usually hostile)
- **Trap Room:** 20% chance of encounter
- **Puzzle Room:** 40% chance (usually neutral)
- **Prison Cells:** 50% chance (mixed)
- **Armory:** 50% chance (mixed)
- **Library:** 40% chance (usually neutral or friendly)
- **Throne Room:** 70% chance (usually hostile)
- **Boss Chamber:** 100% chance (always hostile)

### Determining Encounter Type
Roll 1d10:
- **1-2:** Friendly encounter
- **3-5:** Neutral encounter
- **6-10:** Hostile encounter

(Adjust based on chamber type and party progress)

---

## Notes
- Some encounters may change disposition based on party actions
- Neutral encounters can become hostile if provoked
- Multiple encounters possible in larger chambers
- Boss chambers always contain at least one powerful hostile encounter
